The Journey

While I majored in English, just as one would suspect, I haven't met very much success with my degree. From retail to copyediting, nothing felt quite right so in January 2021 I decided to make a change and throw myself head first into software engineering through a bootcamp program called Thinkful.

Learning a new trade has been incredibly humbling but I'm incredibly excited to start a career in software engineering. After working a year and a half doing Tier 1 IT Support and tinkering with CSS/HTML has paved my way to Thinkful. IT was definitely a way for me to discover a new passion in coding and what better way to cultivate that than to learn even more coding languages?

Thinkful has been a great learning tool to introduce me to the world of programming. I would love to get in contact and start a new project or come onto a team. My skills include frontend Javascript and HTML/CSS
